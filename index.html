Welcome to the STAMP workshop!

This workshop brings together specialists in the spectral theory of linear operators and its applications in various areas of mathematical physics, but also in the wider area of operator theory. With its special focus on non-selfadjoint operators, operator matrices and systems of PDEs, we honour Christiane Tretter for her outstanding contributions to all these areas in the last three decades.
