Visit the Einstein House

The Einstein Haus is a museum (run by the Albert Einstein Society) and one of the many former residences of Albert Einstein in Bern. It is located in the old city at Kramgasse 49. A flat on the second floor of the house was occu- pied by Einstein, his wife Mileva MariÄ‡, and their son Hans Einstein from 1903 to 1905. The five Annus Mirabilis papers in three different fields of physics were written here. During this time Einstein worked at the Federal Institute of Intellectual Property (Patentamt). The living conditions of Ein-stein and his family are shown accurately in the apartment on the second floor with furniture from that time. Einstein's biography and his life's work are presented on the third floor.

Einstein House

The Einstein House is where Albert Einstein lived from 1903 to 1905, during which time he wrote his most important papers. The apartment has been preserved in its original state and now serves as a museum showcasing Einstein's life and work in Bern.

Address: Kramgasse 49, 3011 Bern

Opening Hours: Tuesday-Sunday, 10:00-17:00
