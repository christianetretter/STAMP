Visit the Einsteinhaus

The Einsteinhaus is a museum dedicated to the life and work of Albert Einstein. It is located in Bern, Switzerland, and is a popular destination for visitors interested in the history of science and technology.

Einsteinhaus Museum

The Einsteinhaus is where Albert Einstein lived from 1903 to 1905, during which time he developed his famous Theory of Relativity. The apartment has been preserved in its original state and now serves as a museum showcasing Einstein's life and work in Bern.

Address: Kramgasse 49, 3000 Bern

Opening Hours: Tuesday-Sunday, 10:00-17:00
