Visit the Einstein House

The Einstein House is a museum dedicated to the life and work of Albert Einstein. It is located in Bern, Switzerland, and is a popular destination for visitors interested in the history of science.

Einstein House

The Einstein House is where Albert Einstein lived from 1903 to 1905, during which time he wrote his most important papers. The apartment has been preserved in its original state and now serves as a museum showcasing Einstein's life and work in Bern.

Address: Kramgasse 49, 3000 Bern

Opening Hours: Tuesday-Sunday, 10:00-17:00
