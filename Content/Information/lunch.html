Lunch and Snacks

Lunch

Since there is not so much time for the lunch break, we recommend the following options in the near neighbourhood of ExWi:

  • Mensa Uni Bern (University Canteen)
  • UniS (University Canteen)
  • Grosse Schanze (Restaurant)
  • Main Station (many different options)

Coffee House / Small Snacks

If you are just looking for a small snack or a coffee, we can recommend the following options (in the city center):

  • Einstein Café & Bel Étage
  • Café Lounge Lesbar University Library (has eduroam)
  • Café des Pyrénées
  • Volver
