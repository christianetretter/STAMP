Lunch and Snacks

Lunch

Since there is not so much time for the lunch break, we recommend using the following places for lunch:

  • Mensa Uni Bern
  • Grosse Schanze
  • UniS
  • Main Station

Coffee House / Small Snacks

If you are just looking for a small snack or a coffee, we recommend:

  • Einstein – Café & bel étage
  • Lesesaal in der Burgerbibliothek
  • Café des Pyrénées
  • Café Lounge Lesbar (which has also Eduroam)
