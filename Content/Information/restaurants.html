Restaurant Recommendations

Hopefully you are looking forward to the conference dinner at Restaurant Rosengarten on Wednesday. Until then you are probably wondering where might be a good place to eat in Bern. Here are our recommendations, which might be more locally sourced than the broad tourist information. But be warned that the Swiss restaurants close up their kitchens earlier than you might be used to, often around 9pm.

Restaurant Ringgenberg

A charming restaurant located in the heart of Bern's old town, offering a delightful blend of traditional and modern cuisine. With its warm atmosphere and attentive service, Ringgenberg provides an excellent dining experience in a historic setting. The restaurant is known for its seasonal menu and carefully selected wine list.

Address: Kornhausplatz 19, 3011 Bern

Cuisine: Swiss, International

Special Features: Historic Location, Seasonal Menu, Wine Selection

Visit Website

Schwellenmätteli

A unique riverside restaurant and bar located directly on the Aare River, offering a perfect spot to enjoy the beautiful Bernese scenery. With its relaxed atmosphere and modern cuisine, it's a popular destination for both dining and drinks, especially during the summer months when the outdoor seating area becomes a vibrant social hub.

Address: Dalmaziquai 11, 3005 Bern

Cuisine: Modern, International

Special Features: Riverside Location, Outdoor Seating, Bar

Visit Website

Klösterli Weincafé

A charming wine café and restaurant located in a historic building in the heart of Bern's old town. Known for its extensive wine selection and Mediterranean-inspired cuisine, this cozy spot offers a perfect blend of casual dining and sophisticated atmosphere.

Address: Klösterlistutz 16, 3013 Bern

Cuisine: Mediterranean, Wine Bar

Special Features: Extensive Wine Selection, Historic Building, Cozy Atmosphere

Visit Website

Altes Tramdepot

A vibrant brewery and restaurant located in a historic tram depot building. Known for its craft beers and delicious burgers, this popular spot offers a casual dining experience with a stunning view of the Aare River and the Federal Palace. The industrial-chic interior and spacious outdoor terrace make it a favorite among locals and visitors alike.

Address: Grosser Muristalden 6, 3006 Bern

Cuisine: International, Craft Beer, Burgers

Special Features: Brewery, River View, Historic Building

Visit Website

Kornhauskeller

Affectionately known as "Chübu" by locals, this historic restaurant is one of Bern's most iconic landmarks. Located in the heart of the city, the Kornhauskeller offers an impressive atmosphere with its magnificent architecture and historical significance. Enjoy traditional cuisine in a unique setting, or visit the gallery bar for cocktails.

Address: Kornhausplatz 18, 3011 Bern

Cuisine: Traditional Swiss

Special Features: Historic Architecture, Gallery Bar

Visit Website

Casino Bern

An elegant restaurant located in the historic Casino building, offering a sophisticated dining experience in the heart of Bern. The restaurant combines classic architecture with modern culinary excellence, providing a perfect setting for both business and leisure dining.

Address: Casinoplatz 1, 3006 Bern

Cuisine: Italian classics, French haute cuisine

Special Features: Historic Building, Elegant Atmosphere

Visit Website

Zimmermania

A cozy and welcoming French restaurant in the heart of Bern's old town, known for its warm atmosphere and authentic French cuisine. The restaurant offers a perfect blend of traditional French dishes and modern interpretations, making it a favorite spot for both locals and visitors.

Address: Brunngasse 19, 3011 Bern

Cuisine: French, Modern

Special Features: Cozy Atmosphere, Creative Menu, Local Favorite

Visit Website

Brasserie Bärengraben

A charming brasserie located near the famous Bear Park, offering a perfect blend of Swiss and french cuisine. The restaurant features a beautiful terrace with views of the Aare River and the historic bear pit, making it an ideal spot to enjoy a meal while experiencing one of Bern's most iconic locations.

Address: Grosser Muristalden 1, 3006 Bern

Cuisine: Swiss, French

Special Features: Bear Park View, River Terrace, Historic Location
