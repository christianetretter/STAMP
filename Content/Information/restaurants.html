Restaurant Recommendations

Hopefully you are all looking forward to the conference dinner at Restaurant Rosengarten on Wednesday. Until then you are probably wondering where might be a good place to eat in Bern. Here are our recommendations, which might be more locally sourced than the broad tourist information.

Restaurant Rosengarten

Located in the beautiful Rosengarten park, this restaurant offers a stunning view over the city of Bern. Enjoy traditional Swiss cuisine with a modern twist in an elegant atmosphere.

Address: Alter Aargauerstalden 31B, 3006 Bern

Cuisine: Swiss, International
