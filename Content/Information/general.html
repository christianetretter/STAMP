Information about Bern

Here you can find some general information about Bern, the city where the STAMP conference will be held.

The city of Bern is the capital of Switzerland and its old town is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. We will explore a few sights of the city in our city tour on tuesday. But there is much more to see and even more to learn about Bern. On this page you might find some useful information and inspiration on what to do while you are staying in Bern.
